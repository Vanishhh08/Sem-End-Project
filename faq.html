Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about car insurance, policies, claims, and our services.

General Insurance Questions

We offer several types of coverage including liability coverage, collision coverage, comprehensive coverage, personal injury protection, uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage, and roadside assistance. Each type serves a different purpose and can be customized based on your needs and budget.

The amount of insurance you need depends on several factors including your state's minimum requirements, the value of your vehicle, your personal assets, and your risk tolerance. We recommend liability limits of at least 100/300/100 ($100,000 per person, $300,000 per accident, $100,000 property damage) for adequate protection.

Your insurance rates are determined by multiple factors including your driving record, age, location, type of vehicle, coverage limits, deductible amount, credit score (in most states), annual mileage, and previous insurance history. Safe drivers with good credit typically pay lower premiums.

Yes, we work with multiple insurance companies that specialize in high-risk drivers. While your rates may be higher initially, we can help you find affordable options and provide guidance on how to improve your record over time to qualify for better rates.

Getting a Quote

Online quotes typically take 2-3 minutes for standard policies. For more complex situations or commercial insurance, it may take 24-48 hours. Our agents can provide instant quotes over the phone for most personal auto policies.

You'll need basic information including your driver's license, vehicle details (VIN, make, model, year), current insurance information, driving history, and details about other drivers in your household. For the most accurate quote, have your current policy declarations page available.

No, quotes are estimates based on the information provided. The final premium may vary after the insurance company verifies your information and runs reports. However, we strive to provide accurate quotes that closely match your final premium.

Claims Process

You can file a claim 24/7 through our online portal, mobile app, or by calling our claims hotline at 1-800-SAFE-CAR. Have your policy number, incident details, and photos of the damage ready. We'll guide you through the entire process and assign a claims adjuster to your case.

Simple claims can be processed within 24-48 hours, while complex claims may take 7-10 business days. We'll keep you informed throughout the process and work to resolve your claim as quickly as possible. Emergency repairs can often be authorized immediately.

Not necessarily. Rate increases depend on the circumstances of the claim, your claims history, and your overall driving record. Some companies offer accident forgiveness for your first claim. We'll work with you to minimize any potential rate increases.

Yes, you have the right to choose any licensed repair shop. We also have a network of preferred shops that offer guaranteed repairs and direct billing, which can expedite the process. We'll work with whichever shop you choose.

Policy Management

Yes, you can make changes to your policy anytime by contacting our customer service team or through your online account. Common changes include adding/removing vehicles, adjusting coverage limits, changing deductibles, or updating personal information.

You can cancel your policy by calling our customer service team. We'll need the effective date of cancellation and information about your new insurance if applicable. Any unused premium will be refunded to you according to the terms of your policy.

Digital insurance cards are available immediately in our mobile app and online portal. Physical cards are typically mailed within 5-7 business days of policy activation. You can also print temporary cards from your online account.

Billing and Payments

We offer multiple payment options including monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, and annual payments. You can pay online, by phone, by mail, or through automatic bank draft. Most major credit cards and electronic checks are accepted.

Yes, most insurance companies offer a discount for paying your annual premium in full. The discount typically ranges from 5-10% and also saves you from monthly service fees that may apply to installment payments.

If you miss a payment, contact us immediately. Most companies offer a grace period, but if payment isn't received, your policy may be canceled. We'll work with you to restore coverage and avoid any gaps in insurance.

Discounts and Savings

We offer numerous discounts including safe driver, multi-policy, multi-vehicle, good student, anti-theft devices, defensive driving courses, low mileage, and more. We'll automatically check for all available discounts when providing your quote.

Maintain a clean driving record, consider increasing your deductibles, bundle multiple policies, take defensive driving courses, install safety features in your vehicle, and review your coverage annually. We can help identify additional savings opportunities.
